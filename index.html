Three Ingredient Brownies With Banana

Ingridient Ammount
Bananas 3 medium ripe pcs
Peanut butter 1 cup
Cocoa powder 1 table spoon

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mash 3 ripe bananas. When they are completely mashed, add peanut butter and stir the mixture until smooth. Sift in cocoa powder, give it a mix and transfer to a brownie pan folded with parchment paper.
  2. Bake flourless brownies in hot oven for 22 to 30 minutes (mine took exactly 27 minutes). Let cool completely at room temperature before slicing.
  3. After baking, let the flourless brownies cool at room temperature. Then seal in an airtight food container and store at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also refrigerate for up to 5 days.
